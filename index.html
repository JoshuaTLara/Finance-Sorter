📂 Drop CSV file here or click to select

📝 How to Use

  1. Log in to your bank account.
  2. Find your transaction history or statements section.
  3. Look for an option to export or download your transactions.
  4. Choose CSV as the file format.
  5. Then drag and drop the CSV into the box below or click to upload it.

Note: Your data never leaves your browser — this tool runs entirely on your device.
